==============================
Discipline FX Trading Journal
==============================

Welcome to your personal Forex & Crypto trading journal! 
Keep track of trades, analyze your performance, and improve your trading discipline.

--------------------------------------------------------------
INCLUDED FILES
--------------------------------------------------------------
1. index.html       --> Main app file, open in your browser
2. style.css        --> Styling and layout
3. script.js        --> App functionality and chart updates
4. README.txt       --> Instructions (this file)

--------------------------------------------------------------
HOW TO USE
--------------------------------------------------------------
1. Open the folder and double-click on index.html to launch the app.
   - Recommended browsers: Chrome, Edge, or Firefox.

2. ADD A TRADE
   - Fill in the trade details:
     - Pair (e.g., EURUSD, BTCUSD)
     - Date
     - Trading Session (Asia, London, New York)
     - Direction (Buy/Sell)
     - Entry Price
     - Stop Loss (SL)
     - Take Profit (TP)
     - Lot Size
     - Risk Percentage
     - Emotion (Calm, FOMO, etc.)
     - Result (Win, Loss, Break-even, Pending)
   - Click "Save Trade" to add it to your journal.
   - R:R (Risk:Reward) is automatically calculated.

3. EDIT OR DELETE A TRADE
   - To edit a trade, click the "Edit" button in the Actions column.
     - Update any information and click "Update Trade."
   - To delete a trade, click the "Delete" button.
   - Nothing is permanent â€” you can always fix mistakes!

4. SUMMARY & CHARTS
   - Summary cards show:
     - Total Trades
     - Win Rate (%)
     - Average Risk:Reward
     - Best Performing Pair
   - Pie chart shows Wins, Losses, and Break-even trades.

5. DATA STORAGE
   - Trades are stored in your browser using localStorage.
   - Each browser/device has its own data. 
   - To reset your journal, clear your browser's local storage for this app.

--------------------------------------------------------------
TIPS FOR BEST EXPERIENCE
--------------------------------------------------------------
- Use modern browsers (Chrome, Edge, Firefox) for best performance.
- Keep your entries consistent for accurate summaries and charts.
- Experiment with emotions, sessions, and strategies to see patterns.
- Use your demo trades to test the app before adding real trades.

--------------------------------------------------------------
SUPPORT
--------------------------------------------------------------
This is a self-contained offline app. For any issues:
- Make sure all files (index.html, style.css, script.js) are in the same folder.
- Open index.html directly in your browser.
- Check your browser console (F12) for any errors.

Thank you for using Discipline FX Trading Journal!
Stay disciplined, trade smart, and grow your edge.
